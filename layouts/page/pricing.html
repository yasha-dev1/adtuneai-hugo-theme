Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. The changes will be prorated and reflected in your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my keyword limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or remove some keywords to stay within your current plan's limits.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial on our Basic and Pro plans. No credit card required. Enterprise customers can request a personalized demo.

How does the AI agent work?

Our AI agents continuously monitor your campaigns, analyze performance data, and make real-time optimizations. They use machine learning to improve their decisions over time based on your specific goals and industry benchmarks.
